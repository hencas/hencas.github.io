Writing Samples

Full Chapters' Samples

Once We Were: Prologue - This sample focuses on a narrative that switches between first and third person while welcoming the reader to a brand new world. The usage of a unique formatting system enhances readability while displaying how many creative ways the medium can be used to tell a story.

Once We Were: Chapter 1 - The de facto first chapter of the story, this sample aims not only at welcoming the reader to the current state of this brand new world, but it also comes with the challenge of introducing the main cast of characters. To achieve such, this is a more slow-paced but heavily introspective and determines the writing style and tone to follow.

Once We Were: Chapter 2 - With the worldbuilding done and the characters introduced, this chapter focuses on action sequences and tension based on everything the reader has learned up to now.
