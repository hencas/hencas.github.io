What I do

I offer the following set of freelancer gigs at the moment!

Fiction Book Writing or Ghostwriting.

Always wanted to release a book, but don’t have the knack for writing? Fear not! Regardless of whether you have an idea, I can point you in the right direction and have a full book written within a reasonable timeframe.

I use a 4-stage pipeline process that will ensure you get the best possible outcome for your fiction novel!

And the best? No AI usage at all!

AI Humanization

A more cost-effective way of getting your fiction novel completed.

This service is aimed at transforming your AI-generated book into something that reads like.. Well, human. Instead of editing out AI-words and calling it a day, I’ll rewrite the entire text, using the base text simply as a guide to what each chapter should contain.

Say goobye to robotic dialogue!

Story Consultation

A video consultation aimed at enhancing your ideas and transforming them into something vivid!

The objective of this service is to analyze your book, or your idea for a book, to address any flaws or improve its positive aspects. Pacing, character arcs, dialogue, repetition, structure, voice, and general mechanics are expected to be discussed.

Even if you don’t have a book to be analyzed, this service can guide you on the way of creating one! Let’s craft and polish it together!

  • Learn more

Screenwriting

Are you an aspiring indie director or just simply a more visual type of storyteller? This is the package for you.

Despite being aimed at movie scripts, this service can be used to write interesting podcast conversations or audio dramas! This is often slightly more complex than writing a book, for the simple fact that I put an estimated production budget on the equation when writing, ensuring you’ll have a script that can be brought to life and won’t sit on a shelf forever!
