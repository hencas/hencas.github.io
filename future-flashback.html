Future Flashback

Behold a future where a drug is capable of recreating vivid memories.

Untangle the obsessive mind of an ex-surgeon, that after an accident, starts reliving the memories of a girl he never met.

He is seeing her past, looking into her dreams and unfolding the mysterious future they share together.

Future Flashback is an adventure game set in a cyberpunk universe with a twist: Instead of featuring the entirety of its plot in a crowded and polluted urban environment, it brings the players to the farmlands and forests of a rural area crushed by the expectation of the future while also providing you the dirty, destroyed city we came to love in the genre!

With a storyline that spans the entire lifetime of two people across two different locations, Future Flashback focuses on themes that explore what makes a person their own unique self. Identity, memories, environment interactions, beliefs, and how someone learns about the world are all tools used to embrace the story themes while keeping the player on the edge with a gripping mystery to solve.

During my time as a contractor for this project I wrote the entire story for this project, including developing the scenario, script, levels, in-world text, optional dialogue, item descriptions, and more.

Albeit there is no current release date window available, it is possible to request a recommendation from the project leader (contact me for more info!)
